OG Esports is a renowned esports organization from Denmark, founded in 2015. Known for their success in Dota 2, including back-to-back wins at The International, OG continues to symbolize resilience, innovation, and excellence in esports.
About Us
N0tail

Beyond the game,
beyond the limits

OG Esports is a pioneering force in competitive gaming, constantly pushing boundaries and redefining what’s possible. With a legacy built on innovation and teamwork, OG continues to inspire and lead the way in esports excellence.
OG’s mission is to empower players to reach their full potential, cultivate a culture of innovation, and lead by example. We aim to break barriers in esports, delivering unforgettable moments and inspiring the next generation of champions.
Every challenge is an opportunity at OG, where excellence is not optional — it’s a way of life.

Quote:
"Growing up I really wanted to revolve my life around games, and now I have this house dedicated to gaming—it’s a wonderful thing. As a teenager I sometimes played up to 20 hours a day at weekends, much to my parents’ concern, but that dedication led me to compete professionally by 15 and eventually to Dota 2 superstardom. With the backing of a full support team—from sports psychologists to a full-time chef—I’ve learned to balance my passion with the mental strain that comes with esports."

Johan “N0tail” Sundstein, BBC News interview via The Sun
