OG is a professional esports organization from Denmark, founded in August 2015 by legendary Dota 2 player Johan 'N0tail' Sundstein. The team expanded into Counter-Strike in December 2019, debuting with a newly formed roster under the OG banner.

Maciej “F1KU” Miklas (born May 29, 2003)
From:
Poland 🇵🇱
Role:
Rifler
BIO:

Maciej “F1KU” Miklas is a Polish professional Counter-Strike 2 player known for his sharp reflexes and strategic gameplay. He began his career in the early days of CS2, quickly making a name for himself in the Polish esports scene. His exceptional skills caught the eye of international teams, leading to his recruitment by OG Esports. As a key player for OG Esports, F1KU has helped the team secure multiple tournament victories. He is celebrated for his aggressive playstyle and ability to make game-changing plays under pressure.

BIO:

Christoffer “Chr1zN” Jung Storgaard is a Danish professional Counter-Strike 2 player for OG and a former Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player. He became an in-game leader upon joining MOUZ NXT in September 2022 and tried the role of an AWPer from July to December 2023.

Sweden 🇸🇪
Role:

BIO:

Olle “spooke” Grundström is a Swedish professional Counter-Strike 2 player and a former professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player. He has been active since 2018 and has played for teams like Begrip Gaming, Nordavind Talent, 00 Prospects, and Sprout. Spooke joined OG on January 4, 2025, bringing his experience and rifling skills to the team.

Lambert
Lambert “Lambert” Prigent (born September 19, 1991)
From:
France 🇫🇷
Role:
Coach
BIO:

Lambert “Lambert” Prigent is a French esports professional who transitioned from a player to a coach. He has previously worked with teams like Team LDLC, EnVyUs Academy, and TSM. Lambert joined OG as an assistant coach and was promoted to head coach on April 18, 2024. His experience and tactical approach aim to elevate OG's performance in upcoming competitions.
