OG is a professional Dota 2 organization from Denmark, founded in August 2015 by Johan 'N0tail' Sundstein and Tal 'Fly' Aizik. The team quickly rose to fame, winning four Dota 2 Majors and making history with back-to-back wins at The International 2018 and 2019, becoming the first team to do so.

Indji “Shad” Lub (born October 18, 1999)
From:
Netherlands 🇳🇱
Role:
Carry (Position 1)
BIO:

Indji “Shad” Lub is a Dutch professional Dota 2 player known for his aggressive carry playstyle. He began his professional career in 2019 with ViKin.gg, achieving notable success in various tournaments. Over the years, Shad has played for multiple teams, including Into The Breach, No Bounty Hunter, and PSG Quest. In 2023, he joined Luna Galaxy, where he nearly qualified for The International 2023 and secured a 12th-place finish at BetBoom Dacha 2023. Shad's consistent performances and experience make him a valuable asset to OG's roster.

Daniel “Stormstormer” Schoetzau (born April 13, 1999)
From:
Germany 🇩🇪
Role:
Midlaner (Position 2)
BIO:

Daniel “Stormstormer” Schoetzau is a German professional Dota 2 player renowned for his exceptional midlane control and versatility. He gained prominence with his performances on heroes like Invoker. After a stint with Entity, Stormstormer took a brief hiatus from competitive play in late 2023. He returned to the scene in April 2024, achieving a significant milestone by reaching 12,000 MMR. His comeback was met with enthusiasm from fans and analysts alike, leading to his acquisition by OG to strengthen their midlane presence.

From:
Serbia 🇷🇸
Role:
Offlaner (Position 3)
BIO:

Mihajlo “MikSa`” Jovanović is a Serbian professional Dota 2 player specializing in the offlane role. He began his professional journey in 2019 and has since played for teams like Ancient Tribe, IVY, and Nemiga Gaming. MikSa` is known for his strategic gameplay and adaptability, having achieved significant MMR milestones, including reaching 15,000 MMR in December 2024. His consistent performance and growth have earned him a spot on OG's roster, where he aims to make a substantial impact.

Tamir “daze” Tokpanov (born December 20, 2005)
From:
Kazakhstan 🇰🇿
Role:
Soft Support (Position 4)
BIO:

Tamir “daze” Tokpanov is a young Kazakhstani professional Dota 2 player known for his strategic support play. He started playing Dota 2 at the age of six and began his professional career in 2020. Daze gained recognition with NAVI Junior, where he showcased his leadership and drafting skills. After a brief period of inactivity, he joined OG in February 2025. Daze has achieved impressive MMR milestones, reaching 14,000 MMR in January 2025, and continues to be a promising talent in the Dota 2 scene.

From:
Ukraine 🇺🇦
Role:
Hard Support (Position 5)
BIO:

Ivan “Kidaro” Bondarev is a Ukrainian professional Dota 2 player specializing in the hard support role. With a career spanning several years, he has played for numerous teams, including Alliance, B8 Esports, and PuckChamp. Kidaro is known for his reliable support play and has earned over $41,000 in tournament winnings. His experience and game sense make him a valuable addition to OG's lineup.
