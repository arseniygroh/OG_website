About OG Esports

Transparency is the bedrock of trust, unleashing creativity and empowering each of us to excel as a unified team.

Family is what we're all about. We bring people, communities, and fans together, knowing that through thick and thin, we're stronger united

In our culture, positivity thrives, driving collaboration and support, even through challenges, as we shape a brighter esports future.

We strive to empower and support each other, with the aim of building a better esports industry by placing players, fans, and community at the centre of everything we do.

Founded in 2015 by Johan ‘n0tail’ Sundstein, OG Esports is already among the most popular esports brands in the world. Initially known for its Dota 2 Roster who reached the highest and unmatched achievements possible in the scene, OG is now home to brilliant minds following the same path towards excellence in multiple esports divisions.

A year after our memorable victory at The International 2018, we made history again in Shanghai and became the first team to win the biggest esports tournament in the world twice, securing our legacy as one of the greatest Dota2 team ever. Out of all the previous winners of TI, no team has ever returned to the competition the following year with the exact same roster and even managed to make the top three. Before n0tail, Ceb, JerAx, Topson and Ana, not a single player had ever one this tournament twice, let alone consecutively.
